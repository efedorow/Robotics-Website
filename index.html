About

Lo-Ellen Robotics is a team of high school students and mentors from Sudbury, Ontario. Since 2012, our team has designed, developed and built new robots to compete against other world-class teams. Lo-Ellen Robotics has previously competed at the FIRST World Championships and won awards in business, robot design, team leadership, professionalism and other fields.

FIRST Robotics

The mission of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders by engaging them in exciting Mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills. We aim to inspire innovation and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
