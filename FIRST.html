FIRST

FIRST is an organization that fosters the international development of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math). FIRST gives students and members of the community the opportunity to improve their skills and learn new ones. These skills can then be used to make people realize what jobs they would like to pursue as a career, or as activities that they enjoy.

FIRST Lego League

First Lego League holds robotics competitions for teams of students from grades 5-8. Since we have 12 FLL teams in the Sudbury District, many of our current members have been part of a local FLL team. FLL provides valuable foundational skills that are transferable to FRC (although it is definitely not required).

FIRST Robotics Competition

FIRST holds robotics competitions for teams of students from grades 9-12. We compete in the FIRST Ontario District for spots at the annual World Championships. We have been to World Championships three times since the creation of the, team and we are working hard to attend again soon.

About

Lo-Ellen Robotics is a team of high school students and mentors from Sudbury, Ontario. Since 2012, our team has designed, developed and built new robots to compete against other world-class teams. We have won awards in business, robot design, team leadership, professionalism and other fields.
